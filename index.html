What we stand for and who we are

IGPES is an indigenous company with over twenty (20) years experience in Nigeria. We are an integrated engineering, design, project management, procurement, and construction service provider in the oil and gas, power and petrochemical and chemical sector. We have enjoyed a track record of successful project completion within challenging schedules. Supported by an experienced management team in conjunction with our technical partners, IGPES has proven capabilities to back every project efficiently and cost effectively.

IGPES is working within International standards as concern Quality, Health Safety and Environment, while also recognizing our social responsibilities. Over the years, we have successfully constructed and installed gas plants, gas compressor facilities, and currently undertaking various operation and maintenance services in various service industries.
